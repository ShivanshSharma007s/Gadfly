Welcome to the Gadfly Help Section!

Gadfly is designed to be your Socratic Personal Learning Assistant for Data Structures and Algorithms. Here, you can find answers to common questions and guidance on how to make the most of your learning experience.

Getting Started

Understanding Gadfly: Gadfly utilizes the Socratic method to encourage critical thinking and analytical skills. It guides you through your learning journey by asking open-ended questions and prompting you to explore concepts deeply.

Navigating the Interface:

  • Guiding Questions: Upon starting a conversation, Gadfly will randomly suggest three guiding questions to help you focus your inquiry and stimulate discussion.
  • Input Area: Type your questions or responses in the input area. Gadfly will respond in the style of Socrates, encouraging reflective thinking and dialogue.

Tips for Effective Learning
